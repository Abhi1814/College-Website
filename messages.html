    Chairman’s Message

    The creation of Shivalik College of Engineering comes as a part of our vision. The other closely related aspect of it is to make the institute deliver the types and the quality of services
    you need so that you get benefited too from this vision. The state-of-the art campus with well planned buildings, laboratories and workshop etc. has laid a sound foundation for quality
    technical education to the young minds. It is our endeavour not only to make you qualified and competent professional but also ensure education to be holistic in order to make you
    better human being, prepared for a mature life and make you aware of your responsibilities to the society. You are assured that you will be proud of yourself as a confident and
    successful technocrat after four years at SCE. I wish you all the best.
    Mr. Sunil Kumar (Chairman)
